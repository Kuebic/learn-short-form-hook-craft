# Short-Form Hooks for Online Ministry — Resources

## Knowledge

### Hook craft & the 3-second window
- [Book: _Hook Point: How to Stand Out in a 3-Second World_ — Brendan Kane](https://hookpoint.com/hook-point-book/)
  The canonical text on hooks. Framework: brainstorm boring/controversial/weird → categorize
  (intellectual / emotional / pattern-interrupt) → test on a human → measure with data.
  Use for: the mindset that a hook's only job is to "earn the next 3 seconds."
- [Hook Point — Free "Going Viral" guide (PDF)](https://lab.hookpoint.com/wp-content/uploads/books/HookPointGuideToGoingViral.pdf)
  Free companion. Use for: worked hook examples without buying the book.
- [TikTok Hook Formulas That Drive 3-Second Holds — OpusClip](https://www.opus.pro/blog/tiktok-hook-formulas)
  Data-backed: >65% 3-sec retention = 4–7× more impressions. Use for: concrete formulas + benchmarks.
- [The Complete Guide to Social Media Hooks — Admove](https://www.admove.ai/blog/social-media-hooks-guide)
  Taxonomy of hook types + the 4 cognitive triggers (curiosity, pattern interrupt, self-relevance, emotion).
  Use for: building a hook library and knowing *why* each works.
- [25 Viral Hook Formulas (free templates) — Vexub](https://vexub.com/blog/viral-short-form-video-hooks)
  Fill-in-the-blank hook templates. Use for: fast drafting when staring at a blank page.
- [The Psychology of Short-Form Video — StoryBox](https://www.storyboxhq.ca/post/the-psychology-of-short-form-video-why-hooks-curiosity-and-creativity-drive-conversions)
  Use for: explaining loss aversion / negativity bias / curiosity gap to the team.

### Faith / ministry-specific content strategy
- [Short-Form Video Strategy for Christian Brands — FrontGate Media](https://www.frontgatemedia.com/short-form-video-strategy-christian-brands/)
  Use for: clip-the-best-moment workflow, platform choice by audience age.
- [Grow Your Church's TikTok Ministry — Discipls/ChurchSocial](https://www.churchsocial.ai/blog/tik-tok-church)
  Use for: "connection over virality" framing; devotional/testimony formats.
- [Unlock Your Church's TikTok Power — Vanco](https://www.vancopayments.com/egiving/blog/unlock-your-churchs-tiktok-power-for-outreach-growth)
  Use for: outreach tactics, responding/praying in comments as ministry.

### Funnel: turning viewers into community
- [Full-Funnel Creator Strategy — Influencer Marketing Hub](https://influencermarketinghub.com/full-funnel-creator-strategy/)
  Key idea: move from "rented reach" (the algorithm) to "owned audience" (email/Discord). Use for: the retain stage.
- [Content Strategy for Creators — Ghost](https://ghost.org/resources/content-strategy-creator-funnel/)
  Use for: structuring short → long → owned-audience progression.
