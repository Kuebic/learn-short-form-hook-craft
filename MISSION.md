# Mission: Short-Form Hooks for Online Ministry

## Why
Help people build **unbreakable families through unbreakable faith** — reading the
Bible through the lens of "oneness" (a distillation of Divine Principle). The content
is already proven with a ~20-person in-person group in Mobile; it's too good to keep
local. Short-form hooks are the **gateway** that pull strangers in and invite them
deeper (podcast → sermon archive → website → online study → in-person).

This is not about religious conversion. It's about giving anyone — believer or not —
practical tools for a solid family life, and making that message *findable* by the
people who need it.

## Success looks like
- Pastor + helper can independently write 5+ scroll-stopping hooks for any sermon clip
  in under 15 minutes.
- A repeatable weekly pipeline: long teaching → clip → hook → post across TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
- Hooks consistently hold **>65% of viewers past 3 seconds** (the threshold that unlocks distribution).
- A clear funnel that turns anonymous viewers into named members of an online community,
  and online members into in-person / discipleship relationships.
- The Mobile group's message is reaching, retaining, and ministering to people far beyond Mobile.

## Constraints
- **Two-person engine**: pastor (vision/message) + helper (technical/production). A few
  light-capacity volunteers (camera, chat moderation).
- Starting close to zero on following/content cadence.
- Must protect message integrity — growth tactics never distort the "oneness" teaching.
- Website already exists and is the long-term home; it can keep evolving.

## Out of scope (for now)
- Paid ads / paid growth.
- Debating or defending theology online — the content invites, it doesn't argue.
- Building the website itself (it exists; we feed it, not rebuild it).
- Long-form production craft (lighting, multi-cam) — we optimize the *hook* first.
